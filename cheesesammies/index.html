About Us

Cheese Sammies is Boston’s first farm-to-truck food truck. We source our cheeses, meats and veggies from Massachusetts farms. In addition, we purchase all our bread daily from local Boston bakeries. Our menu is all local, fresh, and made-to-order.

Locations

Cheese Sammies' Truck location rotates daily. Below are our regularly scheduled locations (subject to change). Check our website or Twitter feed daily to find our truck.

  • Mondays: Downtown - City Hall Plaza, Fisher Park
  • Tuesdays & Thursdays: South End - Harrison Ave and East Concord St, by BMC
  • Wednesdays & Fridays: Back Bay - Trinity Place
  • Saturdays: Dudley Square at Dudley street
