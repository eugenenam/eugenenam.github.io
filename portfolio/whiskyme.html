SPARE NYC APP

24-hour Ideation Hackathon to improve Spare's growth strategy


Challenge: As part of the first week of Startup Institute, students were grouped into cross functionals teams to collaborate and ideate. Students were tasked to prioritize with little direction, think creatively, and quickly develop a sound strategy. For this IdeaHack, we were tasked to develop a content/growth strategy to improve user and partner acquisition for Spare while maintaining a $0 budget constraint.
