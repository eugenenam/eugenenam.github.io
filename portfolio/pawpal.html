PawPal

Amber Alert for Dogs


In 2014, my dog Julie ran away from home without her collar on. It was the most terrify moment in my life. I had never experienced a lost pet situation and was panicking. I googled "Lost Pet" and was inundated by the search results. In addition, I felt overwhelmed given that I wasn't sure whether to go outside and search for my dog or to immediately create and print fliers. As a result, I decided to design an app that digitized the process of creating lost pet fliers and notifying local shelters/hospitals.
