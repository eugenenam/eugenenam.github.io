Background

As part of the Startup Institute, I was part of a cross functional team tasked with rebranding and redesigning Mind My Business, a consumer facing app providing NYC small business owners access to open data. Mind My Business was created by Vizalytic Technology Inc., a NYC-based startup that analyzes open city government data and provides digestable insight to local governments, businesses and citizens.
