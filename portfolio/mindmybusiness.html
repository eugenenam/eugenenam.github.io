Mind My Business

Website Redesign


Original Website

As part of the Startup Institute, I was part of cross functional team tasked with rebranding and redesigning Mind My Business, a consumer facing app providing small business owners of NYC access to open data. Mind My Business was created by Vizalytic Technology Inc., a NYC-based startup that analyzes open government data and provides digestable data to local governments, businesses and citizens.
