ABOUT ME

Before joining Startup Institute in New York, I was an accomplished Corporate Banker and Credit Risk Manager. After 8+ years in various positions at ABN AMRO N.V., RBS Americas, and RBC Capital Markets, I realized that my growth in banking was stagnating and needed to make a career transition. I felt the urge to pursue something creative and relevant in today's technology focused culture. After completing part-time courses in UX Design and Product Management at General Assembly, I finally made the decision to leave banking and enrolled full-time at Startup Institute.

I love the potential for learning/growth and the challenges that the culture at a startup breeds. Working in Finance for nearly a decade, I've honed the skills and mindset required to thrive in high-pressure and fast paced environments. I'm looking forward to using the skills, knowledge and resources afforded to me by the Startup Institute to find a career that really suits my ambitions and interests.

My other passions include Notre Dame Football, micro food blogging on Instagram, and training for marathons and cycling tours.
