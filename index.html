About

Background

I started my career in corporate banking — eight years across RBC Capital Markets, Royal Bank of Scotland, and ABN AMRO. It was rigorous and global, and taught me how to think about risk, systems, and operating at scale.

In 2015, I made the deliberate jump into product management, joining VenueBook as a PM. It was a bet on where I wanted to go, not where I'd been. By 2018, I joined what is now Willow Wealth (formerly Yieldstreet) and grew from PM to Senior PM to Director of Product — helping shape a platform that opens alternative asset investing to everyday investors.

Lately, I've been drawn into the world of AI — not just using the tools, but actually building with them. This site is my first project. I'm learning in public, moving fast, and sharing what I find along the way.
